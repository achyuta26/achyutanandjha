Egencia, an Expedia Group Company

  • Hotel Sort: 'SmartMix v2.1'
    • Data Science
      • SmartMix: This was the main project for hotel sort model responsible for displaying hotels on search page based on user profile and company preferences.
      • Since significant percentage of hotel bookings happen within top 10 displayed to users, lead the initiative to deploy LightGBM model, optimized for ranking metric NDCG, for hotel sort replacing RandomForest after thorough comparison of different metrics and calibration curve.
      • The model was A/B tested and resulted in 4% increased booking percentage for bookings within top 3 displayed hotels of search pages.
    • Machine Learning Engineering
      • During the deployment of the model, a lot of discrepancies around feature calculation surfaced in the shadow validation phase.
      • Working with engineering team, four of such features were diagnosed as incorrectly being computed while logging the hotel booking data. This cleared up path for any blockers for deployment in future.

  • Hotel Monitoring Dashboard

    • Initiated a model monitoring dashboard effort with Machine Learning Engineering team to observe any deployed hotel sort models’ performance for hotel bookings made within top N positions of search page across various pivots.

    • The Tableau dashboard is currently in production and used frequently by Product and Tech team and gave opportunity to stem out an analysis for an anomalous observation.

    • Drilling deeper into the online behaviour, Adobe Omniture was used to summarize, in plots, the most commonly encountered web errors across different LOBs and gained traction with Product team to highlight areas of improvement to boost NPS scores.
